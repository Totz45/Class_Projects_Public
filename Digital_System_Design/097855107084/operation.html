Operational Analysis

The following human machine interfaces can be found on the Logitech M310 wireless mouse.

Red: Right click feature used for options or shortcuts feature in an OS.

Green: Left click feature used for selecting with the cursor.

Yellow: Scroll wheel used for navigating long webpages and electronic documents.

Dark Blue: Middle click feature used for opening a new tab in an OS

Light Blue: Indentation used to reveal the battery and USB stick storage container.

Purple: Switch to turn the mouse on and off.
